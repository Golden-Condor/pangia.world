Talk to a Specialist

1. Certified Water Test

We ship a kit or dispatch a technician to collect samples. Labs measure metals, PFAS, VOCs, and microbiological indicators with a full report inside 5 days.

2. Filter Selector

Engineers map contaminants to systems—whole-home, point-of-use, remineralization, or UV. You get a side-by-side comparison and price transparency.

3. Licensed Install

Pangia handles procurement, coordinates insured installers, and ensures the site is ready for plumbing, electrical, and permitting needs.

4. Post-Test Proof

After install we run the same lab test to confirm improvements, then deliver digital dashboards, printable reports, and maintenance reminders.
